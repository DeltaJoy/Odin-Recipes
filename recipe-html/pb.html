Peanut Butter Cookies

Nothing says classic like a good old fashion peanut butter cookie.

John Kanell gives us his famous Peanut Butter Cookie recipe from Preppy Kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 cup of all purpose flour (180g)
  • 1/2 cup butter, unsalted, room temperature (113g)
  • 1 cup peanut butter (250g)
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar (100g)
  • 1/2 cup sugar (100g)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (5mL)
  • 1 large egg
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking powder (3g)

Directions

  1. Prehead oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Sift flour and baking powder together then whisk to combine.
  3. Cream butter and sugars in a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment.
  4. Add peanut butter and mix until incorporated.
  5. Mix in egg and vanilla extract, then add flour mixture and beat until incorporated.
  6. Roll dough into one inch balls and place on bakingsheet lined with parchment paper.
  7. Flatten cookies with a fork in a criss-cross direction.
  8. Bake cookies for about 10 mintues.
  9. Allow cookies to cool completely and transferon baking sheet.
